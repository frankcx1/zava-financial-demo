# Demo Flow Script — Saturday at The Mountain Winery

## The Arc: Capability → Personal Value → Trust Proof

---

### BEAT 1 — AI Agent: Show the Structure (60-90 seconds)

*The goal: prove the AI can act — under control — on the device.*

1. Open app → click **AI Agent** tab
2. Say: *"This AI agent runs entirely on this laptop's neural processor. No cloud. Let me show you what it can do."*
3. Click **Meeting Agenda** → the agent creates a board meeting agenda and writes it to a file on disk
   - *"It just created a document. Not suggested text — a file on disk. This is AI that can act, not just talk."*
4. Click **Review & Summarize** → the agent outputs a plan and pauses with the approval card
   - *"Notice — it stops. It tells me what it wants to access, and waits for permission."*
   - Pause. Let the CEO read the card.
5. Click **Approve** → the agent reads files, summarizes board risks
   - *"Now it executes. Governed delegation."*
6. Click **AI Action Log** → trust receipt appears
   - *"Every action, timestamped. What it touched, what it created. This is what compliance-ready AI looks like."*

### BRIDGE — Transition to My Day

*"That was an AI agent reading and writing individual files under policy. Now let me show you what happens when you point that same pattern at your actual day."*

---

### BEAT 2 — My Day: Show the Personal Value (60-90 seconds)

*The goal: make it personal. This is THEIR calendar, THEIR email, THEIR evening.*

7. Click **My Day** tab. Cards appear: **15 Emails | 10 Events | 7 Tasks**
   - *"Same AI engine. Different job. Now it's your chief of staff."*
8. Click the **Calendar card** to peek → the Saturday schedule drops down. The CEO recognizes the event THEY ARE AT.
   - *"Your Saturday. Hotel checkout, team huddle, driver at 1:15, dinner at the Mountain Winery. Standard calendar file — same format Outlook exports."*
   - Let that land. Don't rush.
9. Click **Brief Me** → streaming progress appears ("Reading calendar... Found 10 events... Scanning inbox... 15 emails... Analyzing...")
   - While it generates (~45s), narrate: *"It's reading 15 emails, 10 calendar events, 7 tasks. Cross-referencing who emailed you with who you'll see tonight. All on the NPU."*
10. Briefing appears. Executive summary at top, then collapsible ACTIONS / PEOPLE TO KNOW / KEY WARNINGS.
    - **Don't talk over it.** Let the CEO read. They'll see their own world reflected back.
    - It names David Park — board member at their table tonight. Warns about the NDA. Connects Sarah Chen's email to an action item. Flags the non-compete clause in Section 7.3.
    - After they've absorbed it: *"My calendar. My email. My tasks. Connected by AI in [X] seconds. None of it left the device."*
    - Point at the footer: *"Analyzed 15 emails, 10 events, 7 tasks in Xs on NPU"*

---

### BEAT 3 — Airplane Mode: Prove It's Local (20 seconds)

*The goal: remove any remaining doubt.*

11. Click **Go Offline** → badge changes to "Offline Mode"
12. Switch to AI Agent → click **List Documents** → it responds immediately
    - *"No network. Still works. Because it was never using the network."*

---

### THE LANDING (10 seconds)

*"Copilot+ PCs aren't better laptops. They're the only place this class of AI can safely run."*

Stop. Let them talk.

## If They Want Even More:
- Peek at email and task cards on My Day
- Show ID Verification tab for healthcare/government audience
- *"This same local agent pattern applies to vision, OCR, identity — happy to show more."*
- *"Want us to set up a deeper session with your team's actual data scenario?"*

## Handling the ~48-Second Wait During Brief Me:
The Brief Me generation takes ~48 seconds. This is fine IF you narrate through it:
- The streaming progress messages ("Reading calendar... Found 10 events...") give natural talking points
- Talk about what it's doing: *"Right now it's reading 15 emails from standard .eml files. Same format Outlook exports. Then it'll cross-reference against tonight's guest list..."*
- Alternatively: pre-run Brief Me during setup and cache the result. Show cached version instantly. Re-run live if you have time.
